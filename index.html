How to Use This System

1. PreviewOpen the weekly organizer before starting the week.
2. LearnWork through the standalone lesson and pause at each understanding check.
3. ReteachIf a check is missed, use the reteach loop before moving on.
4. PracticeFinish with guided practice problems without looking for answers first.
5. QuizUse the weekly quiz to track standards mastery.
6. RepeatReturn on Day 1, Day 3, Day 7, and before the test.
